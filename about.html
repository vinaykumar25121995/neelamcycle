Our Heritage

A Legacy Built in Deoghar

Neelam Cycle has stood as a trusted name in Northern and East India for decades. Born in the heart of Deoghar, Jharkhand, our journey began with a simple commitment: providing reliable mobility solutions tailored to the needs of local entrepreneurs.

Today, we carry forward that legacy into the electric era. Our foundation is still built on unwavering business ethics, customer satisfaction, and engineering excellence—principles that are at the core of every ICAT-approved e-rickshaw that leaves our facility.

Our Journey

Milestones of Excellence

1990

The Foundation

Foundation laid in Deoghar, Jharkhand, starting with traditional tricycle (Rickshaw) manufacturing.

2008

Regional Expansion

Expansion across North & East India, establishing a reputation for quality and durability. Introduce Agra model Tricycle (Rickshaw).

2018

Green Mobility Shift

Strategic shift to electric mobility, rolling out our first ICAT-approved electric vehicles & their parts.

2026

Leading the Revolution

Expanding production capacity with state-of-the-art facilities and a growing national dealer network.
